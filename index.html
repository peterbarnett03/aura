Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 5, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Aura collects and processes the following types of information:

  • Health Data: Symptom logs, pain levels, medication records, and flare episodes that you manually enter
  • Apple Health Data: With your explicit permission, activity data, heart rate, sleep patterns, and other health metrics
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns and feature interactions to improve user experience
  • Account Information: Name, email address, and basic profile information for account management

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide personalized health insights and pattern recognition
  • Track medication adherence and effectiveness
  • Correlate health metrics with symptom patterns
  • Generate health reports for sharing with healthcare providers
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

Your health data is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to any servers.

4. Community Data Sharing

Community insights are entirely optional. This feature may be included in the future. If you choose to participate:

  • All data is anonymized before sharing
  • No personally identifiable information is included
  • You can opt-out at any time
  • Data is aggregated with other users for research purposes only
  • You can delete your data

5. Third-Party Services

Aura integrates with the following services:

  • Apple Health: For importing health and activity data (with permission)
  • PubMed Central: For accessing medical research articles
  • Weather API: For environmental trigger correlation

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access all data we have about you
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

7. Medical Disclaimer

Important: Aura is designed for informational and tracking purposes only. It is not intended to diagnose any medical condition, provide medical advice, or replace professional medical consultation. Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals regarding your health conditions.

8. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
